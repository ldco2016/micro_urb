Grow Food — Not Lawns

MicroUrb is a farm with a vision. Our goal for 2018 is to grow over 50,000 lbs of food on less than an acre of land, using 100% natural, organic methods and only 80 liters of gasoline.

Who We Are

We're a team of dedicated individuals who are passionate about alternative sustainable growing practices.

What We Do

We provide delicious produce grown using organic methods on various rented urban plots throughout parts of Camden County, New Jersey

How We Do It

We use highly effective intensive farming techniques to maximize the production of the land, while regenerating the health of the soil.

Speaking Events

Want to learn more about how we do what we do? Daniel Cortes teaches throughout the world. Check for upcoming speaking events!
